I'm a Full Stack Web Developer trying to put my skills and knowledge to good use. Recently heavy into Javascript (who isn't?) and loving it.

I'm really looking forward to this opportunity as it seems to be a fantastic place to work with a really great mission - bringing local artisans making custom made goods of all sorts into the spotlight as a direct alternative to buying cheap goods from big box stores.

I currently reside in Northampton, MA where I own a small home with my beautiful wife, our wonderful 1 year (almost!) old baby boy Lucas, and our 3 year old Black Lab.
